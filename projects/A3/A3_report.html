Design Rationale for Sketch 1:

  • RATIONALE_1
    • I am interested in the burden of population aging, yet a direct comparison of the elderly population is not as informative as relative to the working population. I was hoping this would communicate the increased burden - with the significant white space between the lines between 1900 and 2000.
  • RATIONALE_2
    • This graph is however overcomplicated. The minimum age for old age is not intuitive and I think might detract from the overall question of just generally is there a greater burden between 1900 and 2000 - but without this axis I would just be comparing two numbers (the ratio in 1900 and the ratio in 2000 - which can't be a visualization alone). The viewer has to think too much about what is being represented, what a higher value means for the question.
  • RATIONALE_3
    • The strength of this is that the ratio is interesting (and the two colors used between 1900 and 2000 show the clear difference). I'd however benefit from a visualization that more clearly shows the ratio visually.
